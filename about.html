About Pawan Traders

Building trust through quality and reliability

Your Trusted Partner in Building Materials

Pawan Traders has been serving the construction and interior design industry with premium quality building materials for years. Based in the heart of the city, we have established ourselves as a reliable supplier of plywood, MDF boards, laminates, and complete interior materials.

Our commitment to quality has made us the preferred choice for contractors, builders, interior designers, and homeowners. We source our products directly from top manufacturers, ensuring that every sheet of plywood, every MDF board, and every laminate meets the highest quality standards.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with superior quality building materials at competitive prices, backed by exceptional service and expert guidance. We aim to be the one-stop solution for all interior and construction material needs in our region.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in building materials supply, known for quality products, transparent dealings, and customer-first approach. We envision expanding our product range and services while maintaining the personal touch that our customers value.
